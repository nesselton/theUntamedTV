WEI WUXIAN

played by

BAI XUEFAN

Wei Ying (Wei Wuxian) is the founder of demonic cultivation and a former disciple of the Yungmeng. Wei Wuxian wakes up thirteen years after his own death when someone named Mo Xuanyu sacrifices himself to summon the soul of the infamous “Yiling Patriarch” into his body. He spends the bulk of the drama unraveling the mystery behind his death as well as bonding with his soulmate, Lan Wangji.

energetic, mischievous, tease, flirt, self-sacrificing, strong sense of justice, outspoken in protection of the innocent, capable of ruthlessness

LAN WANGJI

played by

REN YUTAO

Lan Zhan (Lan Wangji) is the second younger master of the Gusu Lan sect alongside his elder brother, Lan Xichen. In his youth Wangji is somewhat put off my his classmate Wei Wuxian but eventually grows to recognize his feelings for what they are. After Wei Wuxian’s death, Lan Zhan is among the first to recognize him for who he is and stays close to Wei Wuxian, helping him understand the events that led to his death.

stubbornly obedient, strong moral compass, stoic, underneath passionate, perceptive, intelligent, jealous, selfless for loved ones

JIANG YAN LI

played by

FIRST LAST

Jiang Yanli is the elder sister of Jiang Cheng and Wei Wuxian. She is not a cultivator but instead spends her time with the clan, helping with healing and caring for her clan members. She tends to be the one to most easily calm Wei Wuxian down (usually with her cooking) and she is also the peace keeper between Jiang Cheng and Wei Wuxian whenever they fight. She eventually marries Jin Zixuan and together they have a son, Jin Ling.

seemingly mild, enjoys cooking for others and prefers it over cultivation, fiercely protective of her family, selfless, sacrificial, empathetic

JIANG CHENG

played by

FIRST LAST

Jiang Cheng is the blood-born brother to Jiang Yanli and adopted brother to Wei Wuxian. In his younger years, Jiang Cheng spends a considerable amount of time berating his brother for his attitude. Although he feels inferior to Wei Wuxian, Jiang Cheng is continually caring for his family and tries not to bring any hardship down on his clan. He is said to be handsome in a sharp way, with a posture that emits an air of arrogance and overconfidence.

tsundere-type, deep inferiority complex, bad-tempered, cares about reputation, awareness of societal values, deep care for family but has difficulty showing it

LAN XICHEN

played by

FEI BAOYING

Lan Huan (Lan Xichen) is the current leader of the Gusu Lan Clan. He is the older brother of Lan Wangji and sworn brothers with Jin Guangyao and Nie Mingjie. Lan Xichen is very well liked and it’s easy to see why, with his understanding personality and accepting nature - and of course good looks. It not hard to see why he is the most handsome man in the cultivation world.

kind, gentle, polite, treats all fairly, slow to anger, believes the best in people, protective of loved ones

WEN NING

played by

FIRST LAST

Wen Ning (Wen Qionglin) is a member of the Qishan Wen Clan. He is also the you get brother to Wen Qing, and friend/right hand man to Wei Wuxian. In his youth, Wen Ning is rather timid and weak yet harbors compassion for others. He is saved by Wei Wuxian while on a hunting expedition and this incident forms a close bond between the two, making Wen Ning is loyal to Wei Wuxian from then onwards. As Wei Wuxian right hand man, he is known as the Ghost General and is feared by many for his incredible power.

shy, nervous, timid, strong sense of honor, kind, loyal, self-sacrificial, stands up for his loved one, protective (esp of of children)

JIN MENGYAO

played by

FIRST LAST

Jin Mengyao is one of Jin Guangshan’s many illegitimate children. While his other half-siblings may have some sort of status from their maternal side, Mengyao's mother was a prostitute and this seems to make him a target of those of higher status. He serves under the Nie Clan leader, directly advising Nie Mingjue for a time before a conflict sees him leave. He always looks clean and attractive, despite being of a comparatively smaller in stature. Seems to always have the shadow of a smile playing at the corner of his lips.

appears caring and humble, deeply insecure about his roots, desperate for father's approval, smart, ruthless but with restraint, selfish, good with words

NIE HUAISANG

played by

FIRST LAST

Nie Huaisang is the younger brother of Nie Mingjue. Notorious for his incompetence, he is often nicknamed the Head-Shaker amongst the common people and cultivators alike. Huaisang attends indoctrination school at the Gusu Lan clan along with Wei Wuxian and many of the other young cultivators at the time - although he does not carry a sword and seemingly is interested in cultivation as a whole. He has a constantly nervous expressions and seems unsure about everything, owing to his catch phrase “I don’t know.” He eventually becomes leader of the Qinghe Nie Clan.

unmotivated by responsibility, poor student, “I don’t know”, deep loyalty to his brother, intelligent, crafty, cunning

WEN QING

played by

FIRST LAST

Wen Qing is the leader of a small subdivision of the Qishan Wen Clan. She is the older sister to Wen Ning and friend to Wei Wuxian. She has a no-nonsense attitude and tends to commands respect from others. Wen Qing is also a renowned doctor with was taken into the main Wen clan for her healing abilities. She spends a lot of time trying to find ways to heal her little brother and strengthen his spirit. Wen Wing also helps Wei Wuxian in his time of need, even when doing so goes against direct orders from her clan leader.

proud, tough, brave, compassionate, strong, watchful, slightly arrogant expression, deeply protective of and loyal to her family/clan
