Nie Mingjue is the older half brother of Nie Huaisang and leader of the Qinghe Nie Clan. He is said to have a formidable appearances and eyes that seem to be able to burn with rage at a moments notice. He is a very straight forward person who despises cowardly acts and trickery. He refuses to forgive Jin Mengyao, who slaughters Nie Mingjue’s men while undercover in the Wen Clan.

strict, violent temper, protective, black and white sense of morality, intolerant of bad morals, strong sense of fairness and justice, values integrity

LANLING JIN CLAN

JIN ZIXUAN

played by

FIRST LAST

Jin Zixuan is the heir to the Lanling Jin Clan. He is the husband to Jiang Yanli, father of Jin Ling and son of Jin Guangshan (his only legitimate child). Jin Zixuan is betrothed to Jiang Yanli via an arranged marriage between their mothers, something that in his youth he feels negatively about. Jin Zixuan and Wei Wuxian are almost never in agreement and often butt heads with each other. He is fair skinned and considered quite handsome and has a striking figure.

intelligent, arrogant, superficial, brave, insecure in romance, somewhat naive, protective of his people.

JIN LING

played by

BAO YICHEN

Jin Ling (Jin Rúlán) is the heir to the Lanling Jin Clan in the beginning of the show. He is the only son of Jiang Yanli and Jin Zixuan, and nephew to Wei Wuxian and Jiang Cheng. Jin Ling tends to have trouble getting along with his peers due to his temper and prideful attitude. He is described by Wei Wuxian as having learned "all of his uncle and father’s faults but none of his mother’s strengths”. He is friends with Lan Sizhui, Lan Jingyi, Ouyang Zizhen.

prideful, short-tempered, sensitive, wants to prove himself, reckless, adept at noticing emotions of others, stubborn.

played by

FIRST LAST

Luo Qingyang, also known by her nickname Mianmian, is a female cultivator of the same generation as Wei Wuxian. She is a disciple of the Lanling Jin Clan and often is seen with Jin Zixuan. She has skill with medicinal herbs as well as swordsmanship.

logical, strong sense of justice, prioritizes her beliefs over blind loyalty, independent

QISHAN WEN SECT

WEN CHAO

played by

FIRST LAST

Wen Chao is the youngest son of Wen Ruohan, the leader of the Qishan Wen Clan. He is one of the right hand men of his father, Wen Ruohan and carries out his fathers wishes gleefully and ruthlessly. He takes up leading indoctrination of the junior clan members, proving himself to be a hard task master. He carried out various atrocities, from murders and massacres to general mistreatment of those not from his clan. He tends to take on various women as mistresses despite being married as well.

pompous, arrogant, enjoys attention, sadistic, coward

WEN ZHULIU

played by

FIRST LAST

Wen Zhuliu , formerly Zhao Zhuliu is a cultivator in the Qishan Wen Clan who acts as Wen Chao's personal bodyguard. He has an unloveable loyalty to Wen Chaos father, Wen Ruohan. He is known as the Core-Melting Hand due to his ability to melt a cultivators golden core. Once melted, the core cannot be repaired and although the cultivator themselves do not die, they are left powerless as everyday citizens. It is a fate worse than death for all cultivators.

stoic, mild mannered, respect for other cultivators, places his loyalty above his own principles, dislikes arrogant and cruel people
