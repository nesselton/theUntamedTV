The Untamed

Wei Wuxian, a loathed cultivator of "dark and demonic arts", is resurrected sixteen years after his tragic death. His return to the world brings him to reunite with the people in his first life, including his soulmate, the honoured Lan Wangji. Wei Wuxian begins to remember his time before his demise, from his beginning as a young cultivator using traditional tools like the sword and musical instruments, to his very personal methods of using resentful energy of departed beings, with the help of innovative tools of his own creation.

The scheming behind his tragic death is slowly unraveled, along with the mysterious death of other people he knew from his past life.

Source Material and Fanbase

The Untamed is a live-action adaption of the danmei (BL) novel “Mó Dào Zǔ Shī” by [NPC]. The novel was available online for a number of years and gained immense success, eventually having web comic adaption, audio drama and audio book AND an animated series. It was translated into 11 languages and English versions reached New York Times Best Seller list when it was released.

So naturally fans we’re very excited to hear that a live action series was being made. They have been coming up with all kinds of theories on who will be casted and eagerly awaiting official news. They will be very involved and active online as the cast is released and episodes start to make their way out for viewing. If you are casted for a role in this drama, be prepared for the public to be very interested in you.

Glossary

  • Birth Name: Common name; for close friends, lovers and family use; for elders and superiors when one speaks of themselves
  • Courtesy Name: Style name given at coming of age, used by peers as sign of respect
  • A-: Affectionate nickname aimed at siblings or children; e.g. A-Xian, A-Li
  • Er-Gongzi: Young Master
  • Er-Gege: Represents a very close relationship, as Gege is a very intimate salutation

  • Golden Core: Where spiritual energy is stored in the body
  • Cultivation: Manipulating spiritual energy in order to perform a number of otherwise impossible feats (e.g. flying with swords and slaying demonic creatures)
  • Yāo: Creatures formed from living, non-human beings
  • Demons: Creatures formed from living humans
  • Ghosts: Creatures formed from dead humans
  • Monsters: Creatures formed from dead non-human beings
