Auditions for The Untamed will take place in Beijing China, Seoul, South Korea, and Los Angeles USA. While a few people may be awarded roles based on their status, the vast majority must attend auditions in person and fulfill the necessary requirements by the casting director which are as follows:

  • must be able to speak, read and/or quickly learn Mandarin - can reasonably fit the age range of the role.
  • ability to play traditional chinese instruments is a bonus.
  • experience in martial arts, etiquette in a bonus.
  • experience with swords/sword props is a bonus.
Actors of Chinese descent are given first priority in casting, followed by actors of other east asian descent and then finally, anyone who can reasonably fit the role.
